Achievements

Academic Achievements

Throughout his life, Frank has made several achievements in regards to his academic success. Here are just a few:

High School Placement and Graduation

Frank graduated from Union Highschool in June 2013 not only with honors, but as the third highest ranked student in his class of 143.

Fun Fact: Frank was in the running the be the valedictorian of his graduating class, however he decided that he wanted to focus on enjoying his senior year instead of it becoming something of a grind, so he settled for 3rd place instead.

Highschool Honor Band Placements

While Frank was in his highschool years, he also honed his musical talents, and the biggest and most prominent way to express his progress as a musician was to audition for and participate in honor band clinics. Throughout his years as a student musician Frank auditioned and participated in several of these clinics. However the crux of these achievements are when he auditioned for and obtained First Chair in both the county honor band in October of 2012 and all district honor band in December of 2012.

An honor band clinic is where several student musicians spend a chosen weekend doing nothing but working with a guest conductor to prepare and rehearse several (often difficult or advanced) pieces of music to be performed at the end of the weekend.

Acceptance into University and Music Program

Towards the end of his highschool career, Frank decided that he wanted to pursue music in a professional capacity. In February of 2013 he traveled to Blacksburg, VA to audition for a spot in the music education program at Virginia Tech. He would find out several months later that he was successful in this endeavor; not only cementing his place in higher education, but the arts as well.

Graduation from College

Frank's time in university was one riddled with ups, downs, and everything in between. In the summer of 2016, Frank made the (potentially stupid) decision to take 18 credit hours over the break. It was hard, arduous work, however he was successful. This allowed Frank to graduate a semester early, officially earning his BS in Psychology on December 16, 2016.

Personal Goals

Outside of his academic endeavors, Frank is also working on several achievements in his personal life as well:

Getting Physically Fit

For years, Frank has dreamed of becoming, as the youth would say, buff. Despite his aspirations of being physically thicc, several factors would often prevent Frank from making this goal of his a reality. However, in August of 2025 Frank was finally able to commit to improving his physical health.

Improving his Mental Health

As previously mentioned, his mental health was always something of a pain point for Frank throughout his life. However in July of 2024, after a series of rather substantial setbacks, Frank decided to firmly take his mental well-being in his own hands. The road has been long, and it will never truly end, but this is certainly an achievement he has found worthy of chasing.

Learning to Code

The prospect of learning programming languages had always been a daunting prospect for Frank. In 2023 he attempted to learn Python with little more than a borrowed book and a good deal of moxy. Unfortunately this attempt did not end favorably. Luckily he had a wonderful friend who offered to show him the wonders of JavaScript and HTML and on March 31st, 2025 Frank's journey as a developer began.
